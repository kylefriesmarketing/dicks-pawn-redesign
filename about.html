Our Story

From one store in Surfside Beach…

Dick's Pawn opened its very first location in Surfside Beach in 1987 with a simple idea: treat people fairly, pay honest prices, and be the kind of shop your neighbors actually trust. Nearly forty years later, that one store has grown into five superstores along the Grand Strand — North Myrtle Beach, Myrtle Beach, Carolina Forest, Surfside Beach, and Conway.

Today we do a lot more than pawn loans. We're one of the area's biggest sellers of jewelry, electronics, instruments, tools, and collectibles — in store, online, and on eBay. We keep experienced jewelers on staff at every location, every day. And through Dick's Bullion and Dick's Title Loans, we help our neighbors invest, and get through the tight spots, too.

And if you spot Super Dick around town — that's our promise on the move: always out looking for the best deals around. 💎
